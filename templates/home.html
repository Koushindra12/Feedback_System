Help & FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I register as a student?
Click Student Register, fill in your roll number, name, department, email, and password. Profile photo is optional. Once registered, you can log in immediately.
What categories can I use for feedback?
You can choose from: Teaching Quality, Communication Skills, Behaviour & Attitude, Punctuality, Assignment & Assessment, Classroom Management, Knowledge & Expertise, or Other.
Can faculty see who submitted feedback?
Yes — faculty can see the student name and roll number. This system promotes transparent, accountable feedback rather than anonymous submissions.
How do I know when faculty has responded?
A notification banner will appear at the top of your Student Dashboard the next time you log in, alerting you to any new faculty replies.
Is my password stored securely?
Absolutely. All passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's secure PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm before being stored. Plaintext passwords are never kept.
